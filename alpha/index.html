UX Design Leader

I am the Principal UX Designer for Workiva’s Wdesk Document Platform. Wdesk empowers accounting and finance teams to collaborate on buisness-critical data and reports. I lead an incredible team of designers and researchers in crafting the Wdesk Document, Spreadsheets, and Presentations products.

I am also a part of Workiva’s UX Leadership & Operations group. Wdesk is an incredibly large and complex application with a growing number of designers. This group designs team processes to promote collaboration, consistency, and efficiency. I have lead efforts to improve our twice weekly design critiques and establish clear expectations around performance and promotions.

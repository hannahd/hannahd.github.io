Hannah Deering

UX Design Leader

I am the Principal UX Designer for Workiva’s Global Statutory Reporting solution. Workiva empowers accounting and finance teams to collaborate on buisness-critical data and reports. Previously, I lead an incredible team of designers and researchers in crafting the Workiva's Document Platform (including the Document, Spreadsheets, and Presentations products).

I am also a part of Workiva’s UX Leadership & Operations group. Workiva is an incredibly large and complex application with a growing number of designers. This group designs team processes to promote collaboration, consistency, and efficiency. I have lead efforts to improve our twice weekly design critiques and establish clear expectations around performance and promotions.

Crafting Product Vision Stories (Recording)

If you have ever gotten blank stares when describing what you know is the perfect solution, this workshop is for you! Storytelling has been gaining increasing attention as a powerful tool for persuasion and collaboration. Building up a story around your ideas, big or small, helps people understand the problem, empathize with users, and visualize the impact of your solution. In this workshop you'll apply the standard three act storytelling framework to an idea you are working on and walk away with a supercharged way to share your vision.

Beyond High or Low: Multi-Fidelity Rapid Prototyping (Slides)

Rapid prototyping is a way to cheaply build and test ideas. Faster prototypes mean more ideas, more feedback cycles, and more iterations. It lets you get ideas out into the real world where they can be measured and tested. By becoming far more intentional with the way you approach planning your prototypes, you can become far more rapid when it's time to build. Expand beyond just high- or low-fidelity to discover a host of options to better tune your prototype to your goals.
